Privacy Policy - XHS Translator

Last Updated: March 2024

Welcome to XHS Translator's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we handle your data when you use our browser extension.

1. Data Collection and Use

Our extension only processes the text content you want to translate on Xiaohongshu (RED). We:

  • Only process text that appears on Xiaohongshu pages
  • Do not store any translated content
  • Do not collect any personal information
  • Only store your language preferences locally in your browser

2. Data Processing

When you use our extension:

  • Text is processed in real-time for translation purposes only
  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • We use third-party translation services to translate text

3. Data Storage

The only data we store are:

  • Your language preferences
  • Basic extension settings
  • All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • No data is stored on our servers

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we process
  • Delete your local extension data by uninstalling the extension
  • Control when the extension is active
  • Opt-out of automatic translation

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
